Terms & Conditions

  1. Prints will be randomly selected unless preference is specified. If you do have a preference please include that with your address.
    Ex: Portraits, Landscapes, Neon, Weird Stuff, Misprints, etc.
  2. Minimum donation of $2 to cover postage and materials.
  3. Most prints are 4x6 but all IRL NFT purchases may include larger prints. Larger prints are more likely for larger donations. Any donation $10 and up is guaranteed at least one larger print.
  4. All prints 8x12 and smaller are genuine chemical prints from my days at the camera store, printed on a Fujifilm Frontier system. Chemical prints may include misprints and color shifts due to the less stable nature of being printed on ancient machinery using expired chemicals.
  5. Please make sure to include a mailing address along with your donation so I can send your cool new IRL NFT direct to your door.
  6. If you decide to donate more than $50 I will also mail you the nearest object to me at the time (provided it is something I can live without).
